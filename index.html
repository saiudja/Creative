Live pictures

These are pictures of a certain inner state. They are able to transform the viewer in the process of interaction with him, to reveal those qualities that the Divine manifests in one or another of its forms. In the Vedic tradition, there are special rites by which life energy of a certain quality fills the appropriate form - whether it is a statue (murti), or a painting, or any other created image. As a rule, these rites are performed by Brahmins (priests).
The many forms of Shiva include all possible qualities that the human mind can or cannot imagine. Some of them are wild and ferocious. Others are mysterious. Others are attractive and cute.
