Darshan yoga-book

This is a book for children from 6 to 99 years old. The author of the text is Dhroeh Nankhoe, an outstanding performer of Indian music and ragas, a multi-instrumentalist, and a teacher and instructor of yoga for adults and children. This book is a treasure trove of knowledge about the world system, collected and adapted for the Western reader, thanks to a team of singers and editors.

Live pictures

In the Vedic tradition, there are special rites by which life energy of a certain quality fills the appropriate form - whether it is a statue (murti), or a painting, or any other created image. As a rule, these rites are performed by Brahmins (priests).
